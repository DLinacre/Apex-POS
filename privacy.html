Privacy Policy

Effective: 20 July 2026 · Apex POS v2.0

Overview

Apex POS is designed with your privacy as a core principle. The application runs entirely in your browser — there is no server, no cloud storage, and no third-party data processing by default.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Apex POS (products, customers, sales, expenses, settings) is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB, a browser-based database. This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly choose to export it.

No Analytics or Trackers

Apex POS does not include any analytics scripts, tracking pixels, cookies for advertising, or third-party monitoring services. We do not collect, store, or transmit any usage data.

Cookies

Apex POS sets a single benign cookie (apex_cart_active and apex_cashier_name) with SameSite=Lax; Secure attributes to persist your session across page refreshes. This cookie contains only a boolean flag or a name — no personally identifiable information.

External Requests

The following external requests are made by Apex POS:

  • Framework CDNs (Vue 3, Dexie.js, Chart.js, Tailwind CSS, FontAwesome) — loaded only on first visit and cached by the service worker for offline use. All scripts are version-pinned with Subresource Integrity (SRI) hashes.
  • Demo product images — loaded from Unsplash. You can replace these with your own images in the app.
  • Google Sign-In — only loaded if you explicitly configure a Google OAuth Client ID in Settings. No Google requests are made unless you opt in.

Google Sign-In (Optional)

If you configure Google Sign-In, the Google Identity Services script is loaded from accounts.google.com. Google's privacy policy applies to that interaction. Apex POS does not store Google credentials — the JWT is decoded client-side and only the profile name, email, and picture URL are stored locally.

PCI DSS

Apex POS is not a certified payment device and is intentionally out of PCI DSS scope. No credit card numbers, CVV codes, or cardholder data should be entered into the application.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Effective" date at the top will be updated. Continued use of Apex POS after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, open an issue on GitHub or use the repository's Security Advisories for confidential concerns.
